    A Personal Doctor Focused on You

    I am a General Practitioner dedicated to providing personalized and high-quality medical care. My focus is on chronic disease management, preventive medicine, and continuous patient support.

    I believe medicine is not just about diagnosis and treatment, but about building a trusted relationship and supporting each patient with care, clarity, and consistency.

    I provide comprehensive primary healthcare services, including:
